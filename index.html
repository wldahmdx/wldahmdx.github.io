Video Editing

One aspect of video editing that I particularly enjoy is working with match cuts and transitions. Match cuts are a technique that involves seamlessly transitioning from one shot to another based on visual similarities, creating a smooth and coherent flow. These cuts add a level of sophistication and visual interest to my videos. Transitions, on the other hand, allow me to creatively switch between scenes and add a dynamic touch to my edits.
